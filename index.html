Epicodus Track Suggester

An application to suggest a track for Epicous students

Deciding on a track can be difficult for future Epicodus students. Thankfully, the Epicodus Track Suggester is here to help students determine which track is best suited to them. This application will suggest a single track based on the student's answers to the short list of questions below. Remember, this is a suggestion, and each student will need to decide which track is correct.

Epicodus currently offers the following tracks:

  • Ruby/Rails
  • PHP/Drupal
  • Java/Android
  • CSS/Design
  • C#/.NET

Complete the short questionaire below to find the right track for you!

Questions

Are you interested in working with data servers and delivering content to users?
